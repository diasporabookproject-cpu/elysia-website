About Elysia

Celebrating cultures through the art of storytelling

Elysia SAS is a French company that develops story apps for children. We believe that the rise of artificial intelligence unlocks new possibilities for creating amazing content — from tailored stories with stunning illustrations to interactive experiences. We leverage cutting-edge technology to make cultural education engaging, accessible, and scalable.
